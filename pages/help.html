Note:

  • anything inside parenthesis are optional
  • any word in place of colon (:) gets saved
  • anything (i.e. phrase or word(s)) in place of asterisk (*) gets saved

Available speech commands:

  • Turn :on/off the lights
    • example: "Turn on the lights."
  • Turn :on/off all the lights
    • must have a priority of 5
    • example: "Turn off all the lights."
  • (set) (change) (make) (turn) (the) temperature (to) :deg (degrees)
    • example: "Set the tempertature to 75 degrees."
  • (set) (change) (make) (turn) (the) mode (to) :mode (mode)
    • example: "Set the mode to party mode."
  • (set) (change) (make) (turn) (the) volume (to) :volume (percent)
    • example: "Turn the volume to 100%."
  • (set) (change) (make) (turn) (the) light color (to) *color
    • example: "Change the light color to blue."
  • Play *artistOrSong
    • example: "Play Sylvan Esso."
  • Order :number :units of *item
    • example: "Order two boxes of pizza."
  • What's in the fridge
    • Reads aloud each item in the fridge
  • Say *phrase
    • Repeat anything you say.
  • What is the answer to (life) (the) (universe) (and) everything.
