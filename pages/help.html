Available speech commands:

  • anything inside parenthesis are optional
  • any word in place of colon (:) gets saved
  • anything (i.e. phrase or word(s)) in place of asterisk (*) gets saved
  • turn :onOrOff the lights
  • turn :onOrOff all the lights
  • (set) (change) (make) (the) temperature (to) :deg (degrees)
  • (set) (change) (make) (the) mode (to) :mode (mode)
  • (set) (change) (make) (the) volume (to) :volume (percent)
  • (set) (change) (make) (the) light color (to) *color
  • play *artistOrSong
  • order :number :units of *item
  • what's in the fridge
  • say *phrase
  • what is the answer to (life the universe and) everything
